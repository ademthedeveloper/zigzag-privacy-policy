Privacy Policy

ZigZag Runner

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our game.

Effective Date: July 17, 2025
1

Introduction

Welcome to ZigZag Runner, a mobile game developed and published by Dev360. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe experience for all users, including children. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle information when you use our game.

2

Information We Collect

Zero Data Collection

ZigZag Runner does not collect, store, or process any personal data from its users. We do not gather your name, email address, location, device identifiers, or any other personally identifiable information. Your gameplay data stays entirely on your device.

3

Internet Usage & Advertisements

ZigZag Runner requires an internet connection to display advertisements within the game. These ads are served by third-party advertising networks.

Ad Networks: Third-party ad providers may use their own technologies (such as cookies or device identifiers) to serve relevant ads. These providers operate under their own privacy policies.

No Data Sharing by Us: Dev360 does not share, sell, or transfer any user data to any third party. Any data interaction that occurs is solely between the ad network and your device.

Internet Permission: The only reason ZigZag Runner requires internet access is to load and display advertisements. No other online functionality is used.

4

Children's Privacy

Safe for All Ages

ZigZag Runner is completely safe for children. Since we do not collect any personal data whatsoever, there is no risk of children's information being gathered, stored, or shared. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other applicable regulations designed to protect minors online. Parents and guardians can feel confident allowing their children to play ZigZag Runner.

5

Security

Since ZigZag Runner does not collect or store any personal information, there are no data security risks associated with using our game. Your gameplay experience is entirely local to your device, ensuring complete safety and privacy.

6

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your privacy.

  • The right to contact us with any privacy-related questions or concerns.
