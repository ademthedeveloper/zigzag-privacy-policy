Privacy Policy

ZigZag Runner

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our game.

Effective Date: March 26, 2026
1

Introduction

Welcome to ZigZag Runner, a mobile game developed and published by Dev360. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe experience for all users, including children. This Privacy Policy outlines how information is collected, used, and protected when you use our game.

2

Information We Collect

Data We Collect Directly

Dev360 does not directly collect, store, or process any personal data from you. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other personally identifiable information. Your gameplay data (such as scores and progress) stays entirely on your device.

Data Collected by Third-Party Ad Networks

ZigZag Runner displays advertisements through third-party advertising networks, including banner ads and rewarded video ads. These ad networks may automatically collect certain information from your device to serve and optimize ads. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID / IDFA)
  • IP address
  • Device information (device model, operating system, screen size)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, video views for rewarded ads)
  • General location data (based on IP address, not precise GPS)

This data is collected and processed by the third-party ad providers, not by Dev360. We do not have access to, control over, or store any of this information ourselves.

3

Advertisements & Ad Types

ZigZag Runner is a free-to-play game supported by advertisements. The game uses the following ad formats:

Banner Ads

Small advertisements displayed on-screen during gameplay. These ads load automatically and may refresh periodically.

Rewarded Video Ads

Optional video ads that you can choose to watch in exchange for in-game rewards. These are entirely voluntary.

Third-Party Ad Providers: Ads are served by third-party advertising networks (such as Google AdMob or similar services). These providers operate under their own privacy policies and may use cookies, device identifiers, and other tracking technologies to serve and personalize ads.

Purpose of Data Collection: The data collected by ad networks is used to deliver relevant advertisements, measure ad performance, prevent fraud, and improve the advertising experience.

Internet Permission: ZigZag Runner requires an internet connection solely for loading and displaying advertisements. No other online functionality is used by the game.

4

Children's Privacy

Safe for All Ages

ZigZag Runner is designed to be safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. We take the following measures to protect children's privacy:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
  • Ads shown in the game are configured to comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other applicable child safety regulations.
  • Ad networks are configured to serve age-appropriate, non-personalized ads where required by law.
  • The game does not contain any social features, chat, or user-generated content.

Parents and guardians can feel confident allowing their children to play ZigZag Runner. If you believe your child's data has been collected in error, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to address the issue.

5

Data Sharing & Security

Dev360 does not sell, trade, or share any personal data with third parties. We do not have access to the data collected by advertising networks. Any data collected through ads is handled directly between the ad provider and your device under the ad provider's own privacy policy.

Since we do not store any personal data on our servers, there is no risk of a data breach from our end. Your gameplay experience and progress remain entirely local on your device.

6

Your Choices & Opt-Out

You have options to control the data collected by advertising networks:

Opt out of personalized ads by adjusting your device settings. On Android, go to Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization. On iOS, go to Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > Turn off Personalized Ads.

Reset your Advertising ID at any time through your device settings to disassociate your device from previously collected ad data.

Limit ad tracking by enabling the relevant setting on your device to reduce the amount of data used for ad personalization.

Dev360 is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party ad networks. We recommend reviewing their policies for full details on how they handle your data.
